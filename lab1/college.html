Library

Library

The libraries at NIET occupy over 14,000 square feet area and provide students access to a vast repository of resources, including books and periodicals. The peaceful and sunlit areas provide ideal study spaces. Our libraries remain open until midnight on all days. During the examinations, they remain open for 24 hours. Completely automated library management systems make it possible to borrow books at any time of the day or night, as well as make reservations online. Multiple copies ensure that resources are easily available for reference in the library. Trained staff are always at hand to assist students
